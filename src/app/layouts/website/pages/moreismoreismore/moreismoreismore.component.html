MORE IS MORE IS MORE is a nomadic radio show that explores the concept of density as a generative principle of musical form. Accumulation of tension, stratification of textures, and multiplication of gestural elements become tools for the discovery of new expressive possibilities. The notion of “more” transcends mere quantity. It refers to the intensification of contrasts, proliferation of meaning, and the expansion of the auditory experience, where “every detail is full of excitement”.
